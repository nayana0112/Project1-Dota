What Is DoTA?

The Department of Technical Arts (DoTA) is the creative force behind our college’s websites, merchandise, posters, and visual identity. You’ll get hands-on experience working with tools like Photoshop, Illustrator, Blender, TouchDesigner, and more, while collaborating with Crux on web projects.

Domains We Offer

Graphic Design

Posters, branding, merch, and digital art using Photoshop & Illustrator

Web Development

Build and maintain websites with HTML, CSS, JS and creative frameworks

UI / UX Design

Design clean, intuitive interfaces and user experiences

3D & Motion

Create immersive visuals using Blender and TouchDesigner

Video Editing

Edit reels, trailers, and aftermovies that capture the energy of events

This Is Your Chance.

DoTA invites Batch ’24 & ’25 creators to shape the future. Learn. Create. Collaborate. From futuristic event posters to interactive web experiences, DoTA blends technology with art to bring ideas to life.
